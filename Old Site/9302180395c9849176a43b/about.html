Our Beliefs

The basis of all Lutheran teachings is the Bible. We are saved by Grace alone, through Faith alone, on the basis of Scripture alone. The Book of Concord summarizes the doctrine and confession of the Lutheran Church.

Divine Service

At Immanuel, we use the Lutheran Service Book (LSB) as our hymnal and service book. 

Divine Service is Sunday 10 a.m.

The Matins Service is followed typically first and third Sundays. 

Divine Service Setting Three with Holy Communion is used the second and fourth (and fifth) Sundays each month.
